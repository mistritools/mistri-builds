What is YouTube Playlist Downloader?

YouTube Playlist Downloader is a free Windows desktop application built with Python that lets you download YouTube videos and audio in both MP4 and MP3 formats. It's designed to be simple, fast, and user-friendly while packing in everything you need.

The tool supports downloading entire playlists — save your favourite music collections or video series with just a few clicks. Quality selection options include best, 1080p, 720p and more, so you can choose what fits your needs and storage.

No Python installation required — ships as a standalone `.exe` file. Just download and run. Real-time speed and ETA tracking, download history, and dark/light mode are all built in.
